Reflection on chapters 4-5 on the reading. Expensive website, thick interface goes through the problem Scott has with website layouts. Scott mastered the presentation for a website. But overtime there was a repition of style. Instead of typical organization Scott makes a point that all websites dont need to be organized the same way. Websites should have randomness to the layout that makes it unique from the normal. For each and every website in the class there is no template being used. Compression is what makes the form and content of a website, with two concepts within called logality and linearity. The two different modes or memorality can be described as such; logality is a sort of deja vu and linearity is what is remembered over time on the site. Everybody wants a simple website goes through a artistic portfolio and how a web designer expresses it through their own website.
